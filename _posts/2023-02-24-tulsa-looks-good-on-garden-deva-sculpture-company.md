Who might love Tulsa as much as we do?? Mythic Press! Jennie had a chance to chat with owner, Cole Cunningham to hear all about their Tulsa themed apparel and so much more…

Mythic Press is a local Tulsa merchandise partner, working with some of Tulsa’s favorite brands, events and organizations! Starting as a side project, Mythic has grown over the last 8 years to now employee a staff of 20.

More than just t-shirts, Mythic provides an array of services including embroidery, design, events, and fulfillment.

But Mythic is more than all that, they also have a passion for giving back to the Tulsa community, typically in the form of a t-shirt contributing to a cause. In 2022, Mythic had their largest fundraising year raising over $90,000 for local organizations including Tulsa Public Schools and St. Francis.

Right outside of Mother Road Market, Tulsa’s con purchase Mythic apparel at their retail location, Mythic City.
