If you’re looking for a Realtor, you may feel like the choice before you is a little overwhelming. While this certainly can be the case, there are actually many resources available for buyers in our market to find the right agent to represent their needs.

**First, one of the best ways to find an agent is by searching through reviews on websites like Zillow.** Our team happens to have a massive number of positive reviews on this platform, and we’re very proud to have been able to make so many buyers’ and sellers’ real estate dreams come true.&nbsp;

You should always have a thorough understanding of an agent’s or a team’s reputation before deciding to work with them. In addition to this, you should also select someone with a high level of local knowledge. After all, real estate is a hyper-local industry.

{% include pullquote.html %}

**The last, but certainly not least important, piece of criteria you should consider when hiring an agent is their experience.** Contrary to what you might assume, this doesn’t mean just looking at the number of years they’ve been in the industry. This can be misleading. Instead, look at the number of homes they’ve sold recently, as well as their general track record. The agent you choose to help you buy your next home should be one who has a history of success.
