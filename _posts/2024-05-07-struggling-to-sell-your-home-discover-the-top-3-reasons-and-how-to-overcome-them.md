Are you struggling to sell your home? If you’re a homeowner having difficulty selling, know you’re not alone. Fortunately, there are ways to combat this issue, and we’re here today to discuss them. Here are the top three reasons why your home may not be selling and what to do about them:

**1\. Pricing problems.** This is a widespread issue, as many homeowners price their homes too high, hoping it will leave room for them to negotiate down. However, this may end up turning buyers away and losing you money. Today’s buyers are getting smarter, so listen to the feedback you’re getting in open houses, as you may find the common problem is the price. In most cases, you need to lower your price a little, and the right agent can help you do that appropriately based on comparable homes.

{% include pullquote.html %}

**2\. Product problems.** Sometimes, there may be a problem with your home. Again, listen to what people say at your open houses and respond to the feedback. You may need to make renovations or updates. However, sometimes, it can be as simple as decluttering to show that the home has more space. Also, a common problem is staging. The correct staging (including curb appeal, decorations, paint, and much more) will make a huge difference.

**3\. Marketing problems.** The truth is that you won’t sell your home if buyers don’t even hear about it. Make sure you work with an agent who has a top-notch marketing plan. Most consumers start their home search by looking online, so ensure you have an excellent listing. Also, work with someone who knows how to get your listing seen by most people, including through social media and even virtual open houses.

Know that you’re not the first to encounter one or more of these three problems. They have stopped many sellers in the past, but they don’t have to stop you!
