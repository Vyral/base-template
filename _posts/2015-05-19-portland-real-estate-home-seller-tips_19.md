This is also a small pet-peeve that can really irritate some buyers during a viewing. Bright houses always sell. </li><li style="background: transparent; border: 0px; display: list-item; font-size: 16px; margin: 5px 0px; outline: 0px; padding: 0px; vertical-align: baseline;">**Having pets at home. **I love animals, but not when I'm trying to sell a home. They bring along odors and can cause unsightly stains. Remove any litter boxes from your home or kennels that are left out. Try to make it appear as if no pets are in your home. </li><li style="background: transparent; border: 0px; display: list-item; font-size: 16px; margin: 5px 0px; outline: 0px; padding: 0px; vertical-align: baseline;">**Neglecting the exterior: **Buyers are beginning to care more and more about outdoor spaces of properties.
