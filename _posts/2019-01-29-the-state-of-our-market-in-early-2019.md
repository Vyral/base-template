Even though we aren’t yet very far into the new year, **we can already see market trends beginning to take shape.**

For one thing, interest rates are continuing to rise. That said, current rates are still historically low. This means homes are still very affordable.

However, prices are on the rise. **So long as demand continues to outpace supply, this trend will likely continue.** Our market holds great opportunities for buyers and sellers alike at the moment.
