  A lot of clients ask me about the upsides of owning a home. For one, monthly
  mortgage payments are often far cheaper than monthly rental payments. Plus,
  when you rent, you're really just throwing your money away every month. If you
  owned a home, those payments would be going towards paying back the home loan,
  which is a direct investment in your future.
