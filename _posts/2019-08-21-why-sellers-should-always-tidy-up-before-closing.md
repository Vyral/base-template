Selling a home is often a stressful experience. Even so, it’s important not to let your emotional state get in the way of following certain key steps. Namely, **you need to make sure your home is sparkling clean before you close.&nbsp;**

The last thing any buyer wants is to find their new home in a state of disarray when moving day arrives. We know that keeping your home spotless from contract to close is no easy feat, but it’s a crucial common courtesy every seller must endure.
