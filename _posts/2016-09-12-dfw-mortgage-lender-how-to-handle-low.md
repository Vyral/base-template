If youre trying to do a cash-out loan, it could also prevent you from being able to take cash out. <div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-bottom: 0pt; margin-top: 0pt;"><span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
