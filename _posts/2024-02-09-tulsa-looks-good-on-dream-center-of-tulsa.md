Located in areas of Tulsa ravaged with poverty and lack of resources, The Dream Center helps neighbors in need through programs designed to produce outcome-based results.

Since 1999, the Tulsa Dream Center has offered services to address both the immediate and long-term needs within the Tulsa area. The organization's programming addresses four critical areas: economic empowerment, educational advancement, health and wellness, and character and leadership.

Services Include:

**Grocery Giveaway and Chapel:** *Tuesday and Thursday at 10:00 am*

**Hot Meals:** *Tuesday and Thursday from* 11:00am-*12:00pm*

**Medical Clinic :** *Monday 9:00am-3:00pm / Tuesday 12:30pm-3:30pm / Wednesday 8:30am-12:00pm*
