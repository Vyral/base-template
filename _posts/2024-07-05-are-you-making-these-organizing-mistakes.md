Ever wondered what professional organizers really wish you wouldn’t do? If you’re trying to organize your closet, declutter your junk drawer, or tidy up your garage, avoiding these common mistakes can make a big difference. Professional organizers emphasize the importance of starting with simple tasks, like making your bed, to kickstart your cleaning routine. They also recommend avoiding the pitfalls of cramming too much into small spaces, holding onto items just because they’re expensive, and relying on flimsy plastic bins.
