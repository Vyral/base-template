You’re ready to sell your home. Congratulations\! What steps should you take now to ensure that the process is smooth from beginning to end? Your first step should be to get your home evaluated to determine what it’s worth. You can start your search online—might I suggest the home valuation tools at [www.TheMylesGroup.net](http://www.themylesgroup.net). Next, get in touch with a real estate professional, who will come to your property to conduct a comparative market analysis, or CMA, for you. Third, start preparing your home so it shines the brightest once it’s on the market. Add a fresh coat of paint, do some landscaping, or replace your carpet. Finally, it’s time to take your home to the market\!
