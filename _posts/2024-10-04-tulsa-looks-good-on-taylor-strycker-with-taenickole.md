## Tulsa Looks Good on You with Children’s Advocacy Centers of Oklahoma

Carrie Little, Executive Director of CACO, tackles the tough topic of child abuse. CACO supports kids during investigations and the professionals who help them through it.

**The Problem**

In 2023, nearly 95,000 child abuse cases were investigated in Oklahoma. CACO ensures safe spaces and connects children to vital services during these times.

**Self-Care for Heroes**

Carrie shares how CACO provides mental health support for the professionals dealing with this emotionally heavy work.

**Community Support**

Preventing abuse starts with supporting families. Help by donating, volunteering, or backing local organizations like food banks and shelters.

**How You Can Help**

Learn more about recognizing and reporting child abuse at [http://www.cacok.com](http://www.cacok.com) and find ways to make a difference. Together, we can be a beacon of hope for Oklahoma’s children.
