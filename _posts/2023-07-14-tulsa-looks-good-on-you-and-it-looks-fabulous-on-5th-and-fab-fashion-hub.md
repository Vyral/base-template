Tulsa Looks Good on You and it looks fabulous on 5th and Fab Fashion Hub!

Jennie connected with Chantelle Lott to hear about what makes 5th & Fab Fashion Hub just so fabulous.

Located on the corner of 5th and Boston, 5th and Fab is open Tuesday- Saturday and is a collaboration between Lisa Joe, owner of The Stylish Agency and Chantelle Lott, owner of Bounceless.

5th and Fab is a community fashion hub highlighting other retail vendors, designers, and creatives all while bringing fashion to Tulsa in a new and innovative way.

For more information on 5th and Fab visit, https://5thandfab.com/
