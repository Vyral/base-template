Sellers need title insurance so that when the new buyer takes ownership of a home, there isn’t anything that will cause any problems in the title policy. For example, if you bought a home that recently had a new roof installed, and a month later, the roofer says you owe them for it, the title company would step up and pay it. **I’d like to thank Rod for giving us a quick look at what title insurance is used for.** If you have any questions or need more information, feel free to reach out to me.
