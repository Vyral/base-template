## Tulsa SPCA: More Than Just Adoptions

The Tulsa SPCA is a fantastic organization that does more than just help people adopt furry friends. Here's a quick rundown of what they offer:

* **Low-cost veterinary clinic:** This clinic provides affordable spay/neuter surgeries, vaccinations, preventative care, and even some surgeries! They don't require proof of income and the quality of care is just as good as what you'd find at any vet.
* **Community outreach:** The SPCA wants to help pets stay in their homes, They achieve this through:
  * **Mobile adoption centers:** They bring adoptable pets directly to communities!
  * **Mobile medical units:** They offer free or low-cost vaccinations and consultations in various locations.
* **Cruelty intervention:** Their cruelty investigator helps animals stay in their homes by providing resources like pet food and education for pet owners.

## How You Can Help

The Tulsa SPCA relies on community support to continue their great work. Here are some ways you can get involved:

* **Donate:** Visit their website ([https://www.tulsaspca.org/](https://www.tulsaspca.org/) ) to donate financially or with in-kind items like pet food or leashes.
* **Volunteer:** They have flexible volunteer opportunities, You can even foster a pet temporarily!
* **Adopt:** They're currently full due to recent storms, but by adopting, you'll free up space for them to help more animals!

Even if you can't adopt right now, consider sharing this information with others who might be interested. Every bit helps the Tulsa SPCA find loving homes for these animals!
