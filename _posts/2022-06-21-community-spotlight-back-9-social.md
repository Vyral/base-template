In today’s small business spotlight, we’ll be talking about Back 9 Social in Wallingford, Connecticut.

There are a lot of these golf simulator places, but Back 9 Social adds 40 years of hospitality experience to the mix. The space is an old warehouse, but you'll be impressed when you step inside. **Whether you're a 30-handicap, a two-handicap, or you've never even swung a golf club, you can come in and enjoy the experience.** They serve handcrafted cocktails and great food, so even if you’re not a golfer, you will have a good time.&nbsp;

Surrounding the bar are 10 golfing bays with couches where you can relax and have fun with friends. Trackman technology provides awesome data to help you improve your swing. You don't have to go to New York to get a great golf experience—check out Back 9 Social.

If you ever have any questions, don’t hesitate to reach out to us by phone or email. We look forward to hearing from you.
