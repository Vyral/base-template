It's hard to believe that the summer is nearly over. We thought that now would be a great time to give you the latest real estate market update for Los Angeles so you can know what's going on. For some reason, people think that the market slows down in the fall because of the weather, but we are in LA, and we really don't have your typical fall (or winter for that matter), so our selling season never really stops.
