Welcome to fifty58, a gorgeous building located at 58 Midland that contains 45 dwellings, 18 of which are two-story duplexes, and the rest are 2-bedroom and 2-bathroom condos.&nbsp;

**All of the kitchens at 58 Midland feature Padini cabinetry, cutting-edge black slate GE appliances with a gas range, plus a fluid and dramatic waterfall countertop with natural stone quartz.**

The generous, oversized master bedroom suite has enough space to lounge on your king size bed, or just hang out and read a book in a chair. **All master bedrooms feature a large walk-in closet and an oversized, luxury master bathroom with double vanities and a large glass frameless door shower.**

All of the units at 58 Midland have their own washer and dryer, along with 9-foot ceilings, recessed lighting, and beautiful architectural detail in every room. **This building will surpass your expectations and is nothing like any other building you’ve seen.**
