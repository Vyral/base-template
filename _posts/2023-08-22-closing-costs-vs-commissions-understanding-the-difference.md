Have you ever found yourself wondering, "What are closing costs?" It's a common question that often gets confused with real estate commissions. Allow us to shed light on this topic and provide you with a clear understanding.

Closing costs refer to the fees you pay to your lender to facilitate the origination of your loan. While the average closing cost typically falls between one and 2%, **the exact percentage can vary from lender to lender and situation to situation.**

{% include pullquote.html %}

These costs encompass a range of charges, including processing fees, appraisal fees, title fees, and more. Lenders may charge different amounts for these services, and they all contribute to the overall closing costs.

Closing costs are an essential factor to consider as a homebuyer. These costs are usually the responsibility of the buyer and are necessary to finalize the property purchase. However, in some cases, **it's possible to negotiate with the seller to have them cover these expenses as part of the agreement.**
