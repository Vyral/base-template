You can help CREE Cares give back to those in need.

Today we want to spread the word about CREE Cares, our team’s compassion program dedicated to assisting those in need.&nbsp;

If you know anyone in our community who’s been struggling financially due to COVID or any other factors, we’re here to help. For every transaction a member of our team makes, a portion of our company commission will be allocated to an anonymous donation fund for those in need.

{% include pullquote.html %}

**All you have to do is visit [creecares.com](http://creecares.com){: target="_blank" rel="noopener"} and fill out an application for anyone you know who could use our assistance.** Their information will be kept strictly confidential. If you’d like to donate to this fund, send us an email and we’ll get back to you with further instructions.&nbsp;

As always, if you have questions about this subject or there’s anything else we can help you with, feel free to reach out to us as well. We’d love to hear from you.
