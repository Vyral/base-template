Black Moon Collective was establish in 2018 and made up of a collective of 11 artists from all over, now residing in Tulsa.

The goal of the collective is breaking standards, pushing innovation, and cultivating creativity in their community.

As a group of individual arts, the goal is to be a channel that brings unity, awareness, and hope to neglected parts of the community, by making art accessible and viewable to all.

Black Moon Collective also offers a variety of class for all ages .
