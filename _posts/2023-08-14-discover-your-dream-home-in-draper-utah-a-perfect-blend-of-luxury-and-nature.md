One of the first things that catches people's attention about Draper is its average income of approximately $155,000. This thriving community has garnered attention far and wide, earning its place as No. 18 on the list of best cities to live in across the nation. **With a strong economy and an ever-growing job market, Draper presents excellent opportunities for career growth and stability.**

Draper's real estate offerings not only provide comfort but also contribute to the city's appeal as a thriving residential hub.
