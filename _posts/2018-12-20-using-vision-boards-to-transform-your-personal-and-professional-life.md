Using vision boards is something I have personally been doing for many, many years, and I absolutely love it. **It has had a huge impact on my life and career, and it can do the same for you.&nbsp;**

The technique of using vision boards has been so helpful for me that I’ve decided to share it with my team during our next meeting. I can’t wait to show my team members how to create their own vision boards, and this got me thinking—I’d like to share this technique with all of you, as well.

{% include pullquote.html %}

You’ve got to dream big to see big results in your life and your career. Creating a vision board is as simple as compiling visual elements in a single place to encourage a goal. However, the secret behind a successful vision board is that you have to do it with intention. **You can’t simply create a board and wait for results.** You need to carry the intention to succeed in your heart as you move forward toward your goals.&nbsp;

So when you consider your own goals, also think about what you’re doing to give them purpose and to make them happen. It’s awesome to dream big, but it’s even more awesome to realize those dreams.
