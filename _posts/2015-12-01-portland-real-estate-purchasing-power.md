Indefinitely, owning a home has a dramatic impact on your familys net worth in the economy.

1. <span style="font-size: normal;"><span style="font-family: &quot;arial&quot; , &quot;helvetica&quot; , sans-serif;">**People want to choose the environment they live in.** We conduct business with clients we admire.
