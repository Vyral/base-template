ABOUT AARON BOWMAN

Joining me today is real estate investor Aaron Bowman. Though his initial path wasn’t in real estate, he made a transition around three years ago and has since found faster-than-normal success in this industry.

You’ll learn how Aaron Bowman took initiative to build his own Real Estate business and how you can model his approach to building your business with an amazing sales team. Over time he has developed a script and training program to implement by overcoming multiple challenges in the real estate industry. Aaron also discusses how he convinces sellers to transact for less money by building the relationship and asking five basic questions.&nbsp;

KEY POINTS

* How he leverages his strategy to get sellers to transact for less money
* What his process is from receiving lists to the follow-up
* How he establishes timelines for sellers who aren’t yet ready
* A look at how Aaron organizes his lead generation and follow-up
