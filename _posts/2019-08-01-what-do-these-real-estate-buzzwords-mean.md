If you’ve been driving down the highway or listened to the radio recently, you have probably seen a few repeated real estate buzzwords like “iBuyer” or “Guaranteed Offer” or “How to Net More With X Agent.”

**There’s a ton of noise out there in the market, but what can you believe and trust?** It’s important to be able to sit down and discuss these programs and options. We can help you cut through the noise and figure out what’s behind these buzzwords and promises. Many of these terms create good marketing, but it takes a true professional to give you the honest truth about if these programs are a cost, or a benefit to you.

{% include pullquote.html %}

**The key is to look at things from a local perspective.** A lot of these messages are very broad. An agent who knows the local market where your home is located will be an invaluable tool throughout the process.
