The real estate market has been hot for several months now, and it will probably remain that way for a while. Here are three great tips to help you sell your home quickly and for top dollar in today’s real estate market:

**1\. Don’t just declutter, deep clean your home too.** Too many people think they can simply box up some stuff and they’re good to go. A decluttered home can still feel dirty, and that’s what you want to avoid. Today’s buyers are very cognizant of the cleanliness of their environment even if they aren’t physically touching things. Spend a few extra bucks and have your house cleaned or just roll up your sleeves and do it yourself.

{% include pullquote.html %}

**2\. Perfect preparation makes for a perfect home sale.** Typically, our clients who need to buy and sell need at least a couple days, if not a couple weeks, to prepare. We want to create an environment similar to new construction homes.

**3\. Hire a top agent.** You need an agent who will not only be honest with you, but will also guide you through the entire process to close quickly and sell for top dollar.
