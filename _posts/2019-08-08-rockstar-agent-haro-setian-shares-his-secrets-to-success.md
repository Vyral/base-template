On the latest episode of Agent Success Secrets, I had the pleasure of speaking to Greenville, South Carolina Realtor Haro Setian. Haro came to Keller Williams in 2009, when the market was, of course, not in the best state. Despite entering the industry at a difficult time, Haro has absolutely thrived since then. Now, he’s going to explain how he did it.
