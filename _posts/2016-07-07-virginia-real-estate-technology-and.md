We just got back from a fantastic real estate conference. We learned all the latest things happening in the real estate world in regards to new technology and how the team structure is changing the business.

This trip brought up an important point. If your Realtor doesn't’ spend the time to get educated and trained in these new areas of the business on a regular basis, you are missing out. It’s essential for growth, especially in a market that’s changing as fast as this one is.

In the last few years, the tools and new education in real estate have helped to drive an amazingly better client experience. You want your Realtor to be getting fresh ideas and continuing their education. That way they can pack those tools in their toolkit and use them to your advantage when you’re buying or selling.
