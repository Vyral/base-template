Your interest rate and annual percentage rate (or APR) will never match. While your interest rate is the set figure your mortgage rate is calculated on, your APR signifies the amount your lender will earn over the life of your loan. Origination fees, discount points, and private mortgage insurance will each factor into your APR. To learn more about this topic, watch my latest video.
