<font data-blogger-escaped-style="font-family: &quot;arial&quot; , &quot;helvetica&quot; , sans-serif;"><font face="&quot;arial&quot; , &quot;helvetica&quot; , sans-serif">Many homeowners want to know what their home is worth before they put it on the market. How do buyers determine the value of your home? In other words, how is market value determined?</font></font>

<br>

<font data-blogger-escaped-style="font-family: &quot;arial&quot; , &quot;helvetica&quot; , sans-serif;"><font face="&quot;arial&quot; , &quot;helvetica&quot; , sans-serif">First, though, there are a few misconceptions about home value that I would like to clear up:</font></font>

* <font data-blogger-escaped-style="font-family: &quot;arial&quot; , &quot;helvetica&quot; , sans-serif;"><font face="&quot;arial&quot; , &quot;helvetica&quot; , sans-serif">Home value is not based on what you want or need to pay off your loan amount.&nbsp;</font></font>
* <font data-blogger-escaped-style="font-family: &quot;arial&quot; , &quot;helvetica&quot; , sans-serif;"><font face="&quot;arial&quot; , &quot;helvetica&quot; , sans-serif">Home value is not based on what your neighbors think you should get for your home. After all, neighbors are biased.&nbsp;</font></font>
* <font data-blogger-escaped-style="font-family: &quot;arial&quot; , &quot;helvetica&quot; , sans-serif;"><font face="&quot;arial&quot; , &quot;helvetica&quot; , sans-serif">Your tax assessed value does not reflect market value. Tax assessed values can be off by as much as 20%.&nbsp;</font></font>
* <font data-blogger-escaped-style="font-family: &quot;arial&quot; , &quot;helvetica&quot; , sans-serif;"><font face="&quot;arial&quot; , &quot;helvetica&quot; , sans-serif">The appraised value is not the same as market value. If you have an appraisal that is a year old, it is based on outdated information.&nbsp;</font></font>
* <font data-blogger-escaped-style="font-family: &quot;arial&quot; , &quot;helvetica&quot; , sans-serif;"><font face="&quot;arial&quot; , &quot;helvetica&quot; , sans-serif">Home value is different from your insurance value. Insurance companies insure the house for the rebuilding or replacement cost, which is much higher than market value.&nbsp;</font></font>
* <font data-blogger-escaped-style="font-family: &quot;arial&quot; , &quot;helvetica&quot; , sans-serif;"><font face="&quot;arial&quot; , &quot;helvetica&quot; , sans-serif">Sites like Zillow cannot determine home value with just a computer algorithm.&nbsp;</font></font>

<br>

<font data-blogger-escaped-style="font-family: &quot;arial&quot; , &quot;helvetica&quot; , sans-serif;"><font face="&quot;arial&quot; , &quot;helvetica&quot; , sans-serif">Home buyers determine the market value of your property by looking at the features and benefits of your home (how many bedrooms, bathrooms, square footage, etc.) and compare your property to other homes on the market. They look at recently sold properties similar to yours to see what your home is worth.</font></font>
