How can you max out your home’s value? There are a couple of ways, and which one you choose will depend on how much work you want to do. As a side note, most of this doesn’t really apply to newer houses.

You can focus on improving the value of one room. **This can mean doing stuff as small as decluttering and opening up space.** This way, people can better see what the room offers. You could go a little further and upgrade the flooring or paint the walls. If you have a darker color of paint, re-painting with a lighter color can help maximize the room’s value.

You can also focus on the value of your house as a whole. **This involves renovating kitchens, bathrooms, living spaces, or the outside space.** The focus should definitely be on the kitchens and bathrooms, though.
