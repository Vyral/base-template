Jennie recently connect with Kuma Roberts, the President and CEO of the Black Wall Street Chamber of Commerce in Tulsa, OK!

**Kuma's Passion: Bridging the Racial Wealth Gap**

Kuma, a proud daughter of Tulsa, is dedicated to empowering the African American community and closing the racial wealth gap. She highlights the importance of remembering the historical legacy of Greenwood, a once-thriving Black neighborhood destroyed in the 1921 Tulsa Race Massacre.

**The Black Wall Street Chamber's Mission**

The Chamber's mission is to foster economic growth for Black businesses. They achieve this through:

* **Education:** Programs like the Nest Collective help entrepreneurs develop business plans.
* **Empowerment:** They encourage individuals with side hustles to see themselves as business owners.
* **Opportunity:** They connect Black-owned businesses with contracting opportunities.

**How You Can Help**

The Black Wall Street Chamber welcomes your support! Here's how you can get involved:

* **Become a Member:** Visit their website ([http://bwschamber.com](http://bwschamber.com) ) to join as an individual, non-profit, or corporation.
* **Spread the Word:** Share the Chamber's mission and story with your network.

**A Bright Future for Tulsa**

Kuma emphasizes Tulsa's positive growth and the thriving Black business community. The Chamber's collaborative approach ensures success for all of Tulsa. Join them in creating a future that builds upon Greenwood's legacy!
