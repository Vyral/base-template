Millennials currently make up the largest generation of homebuyers in our market, so it’s important that you know what they’re looking for in a home. Here’s how to attract them to your listing:

**1\. Be technologically friendly.** Have hookups for electronics such as TVs, entertainment centers, and computers. In addition, consider installing thermostats and security systems that can be accessed via smartphone.

{% include pullquote.html %}

**2\. Leave room for DIY.** Let them add their own personality to the home. Lower your price and leave some of the easy fixes for them to do.

**3\. Go green.** Use environmentally friendly paint with fewer toxins. Consider installing energy-efficient appliances and light bulbs as well.
