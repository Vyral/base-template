This is huge news, as it means our market will continue to thrive with activity.
Let's take a look at prices - where are they going? Well, a new FHFA report shows that home prices are going up significantly across the country. The Middle Atlantic Region, where we're located, is seeing price increases at a lower rate than any other region. This is actually a good thing, because it means our market has stabilized more quickly than the rest of the country.
We're currently in a very good place as far as prices are concerned. Additionally, we are seeing strong signs that our sales volume is in a solid place as well. Our market is coming back to a much more normalized status, which is great news for everyone in our community.
