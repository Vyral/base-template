How to Have a Smooth Loan Process

When you decide to buy a home, having a great experience with a loan officer will go a long way in making the whole process easier. Today we are joined by Suzi Cooper, a loan officer for Wells Fargo. She has been working in the business for 22 years and knows the things that will help buyers to have a smooth loan experience. 

According to Suzi, the most important thing to do in the loan process is get pre-approved. It's best to get pre-approved before you even start looking at homes, if at all possible. If you wait to get pre-approved, you could risk losing out on homes. You could find the perfect home, but if you aren't pre-approved, the seller may go in a different direction. We come across buyers all the time who don't get pre-approved because they don't think they will find something. When they don't find anything, it's a much more difficult process. 

The pre-approval process is pretty straightforward for Suzi. She looks through 2 years of income, bank statements, and employment situations and if she submits your loan, you know you are pre-approved.
