“We had two different offers on the house and the first one unfortunately fell through. The buyer, we thought they could get qualified but they were unable to get qualified. The second one did go through completion, so we sold it with minimal time. He met with us a year and a half ago about whether or not the property could be sold and she could walk away. It was my mother in law’s home. Unfortunately, at the time, the market would not support her high mortgage balance. We waited a year and I kept in touch with him and when we realized she couldn’t live independently anymore we called him again. He came to the home and each time he provided a really nice documented book of the area with pictures. It was a really nice set up, even though he didn’t have my business yet. On the second one he came through gave us some recommendations we could do to improve the value of the home. We worked on that for a couple of months and then called him and back and signed with him to list it. “

-Heather
