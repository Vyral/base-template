March 2014 Birmingham Market Update

For this installment of my blog, I'm going to get you updated on the market here in Birmingham so you can hit the ground running this spring when the market begins to bloom.

  • In the past 30 days, there was a 9% decrease in the amount of homes sold.
    • However, there was a 3% increase in the selling price of those homes. 
  • This leads me to believe that our market is rebounding, and I expect a good year for us here in Birmingham.
    • If you've had trouble selling your home in the past, then this might be a lucky year for you. Low interest rates will be spurring buyers to purchase homes this year, so I expect to see a lot of market activity on both sides.
  • Property values are still not back up to what they used to be a few years ago, but once again this will cause buyers to purchase homes. It's not a sterling situation for sellers, but if you've been having trouble selling, then this could actually aid you.
  • Inventory has steadily been decreasing and pushing our Birmingham market towards a more neutral market.
    • I expect in the next 12-18 months to see the market shift again towards a seller's market as inventory decreases.
      • So, if you're looking to buy a home, you'll want to do it soon. 
So whether you're buying or selling here in Birmingham, you're going to find a market that will suit your needs. Sellers will be happy with a relatively low inventory that will bring them multiple offers, and buyers will be happy with lower mortgage rates and home values.
