There are millions of real estate agents out there, but there aren’t that many actual Realtors. Realtors subscribe to a code of ethics and conduct and belong to the National Association of Realtors. You need to make sure that the person you are hiring is an experienced Realtor rather than an agent who took a few classes at real estate school and got certified. Here are five important tasks your Realtor needs to be able to do that they simply don’t teach in real estate school:

1. How to set the price of your home. This is 80% of the home selling battle and it’s a fine art coming up with a proper figure.

2. How to write an offer for a buyer. This is a complex process, and not something you can learn in a few classes. I even run into Realtors who still don’t understand completely how to make an offer.

3. How to negotiate an offer for a seller. You can’t just pick the offer with the highest price; you’ve got to look at all the terms. An experienced agent will be able to handle multiple offers and help you pick the best one for you.

4. How to help a seller get their house ready for market. Whether it’s through decluttering, choosing neutral paint colors, or staging the home to sell, an experienced Realtor will know what you need to do in order to help your home show well and receive top dollar offers.

5. How to market a house for sale. The marketing for your home needs to be done with excellence and accuracy. You need to be able to highlight the unique features of your home while positioning it online where the most people will see it.
