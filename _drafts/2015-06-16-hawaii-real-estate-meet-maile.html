As an experienced real estate investor working in Finance, Maile Kekona (Tsurusato) transitioned Real Estate from part time hobby to full time profession. As a native Hawaiian, with multi- generation Maui roots, Maile is committed to Maui County with additional focus on Big Island.

After graduating as a collegiate athlete from Northwood University, Maile entered the banking world and was instantly at home with numbers. Her career grew quickly as a result of focusing on investment property development and passive income generation. This fueled her interest in understanding personal finance, and propelled her to earn her Mortgage License, Insurance License, and now Real Estate License.

As a mortgage free home owner, and a mother of two, Maile is committed to seeing that everyone on Maui has a choice to buy or invest in real estate as a means to truly establishing wealth. Although her focus is sharp, her team player attitude shines through to communicate easily, and negotiate effectively. 

Maile spends her free time camping, and in the ocean enjoying the simple pleasures of life on Maui with her young son and daughter. Occasionally that trip includes their dog Kuma, 160 lbs St. Mastiff. Her goal is to strengthen buyers, nurture investors, and simplify the selling process.
