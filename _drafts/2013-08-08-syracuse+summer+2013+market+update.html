Hello everyone! Welcome to my video real estate blog!

What’s happening in our current real estate market?

In the beginning of this year, we saw the market taking off. Buyers were finding a lot of homes that they liked and they were buying them.  There wasn’t a large amount of inventory for them to choose from, though.

Right now, in July, we are seeing the market slow down some.  Year-to-date, we are up 2% over last year in the amount of closed transactions. That’s pretty much a flat market.

On the national level, we are hearing the market is up 8-12%, that’s not the Syracuse area. We never lead a trend; we always follow.

Statistics show that our pending sales are down 10% from last year. Now, I don’t want to be a negative person; that could just be a down month for our market.

What we are looking at now, though, is a better July than we had last year. That’s good for us, but we are really seeing a slowing down of buyers.

August can be the third slowest month of the year; January and February are the two slowest. Why is this? Kids go back to school; families go on vacation, and a lot of other activities are going on. So, July is a great time to make your real estate move.
