 What is the Difference Between an Active and Passive Agent

The difference with an active agent is that they will be out prospecting for your buyers. I will actually pre-market your home before it even hits the market. I belong to a group of a about 20 tops agents here in Anchorage and before your home reaches the MLS, I have already given them details about your property. I will price watch to see what is going on in your neighborhood and also watch your comparables to make sure that we’re on the right track for pricing.

What Separates Top Agents from Others 
 
One of the things that a larger agent has the ability to do is offer multiple websites. We offer our clients 250-500 different websites that your property will go out to. Websites are so important in today’s market; 93% of all buyers come from the internet! This leads me to my last point which is how I will properly stage your home. I will make sure it’s prepared for showings by having it neat, clean and organized. Most buyers buy homes off of emotion and having pictures of a clean home will get buyers from the internet into your home.
