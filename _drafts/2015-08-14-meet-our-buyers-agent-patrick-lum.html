Interested in a career in Hawaii real estate? Team Lally is always looking for superstar salespeople and dedicated admin staff for our real estate team. Email or call us at (808) 426-7211 for a FREE consultation to answer any of your real estate career questions


Patrick grew up in Honolulu, graduated from The Kamehameha Schools, and earned a Hotel Management degree from the University of Nevada, Las Vegas.

While in Las Vegas, he developed a passion for real estate and purchased his first home.

After working at various resorts and casinos throughout the U.S., Canada, and Australia, he learned the importance of strong communication and how to deliver the best in five-star customer service.

After working for The Ritz-Carlton in Las Vegas, he followed the luxury brand to Washington, D.C., where he learned from one of the most dynamic sales teams in the business. He then entered the field of residential property management, led sales teams at multiple Class A communities, and earned a Sales Associate of the Quarter award, among 100+ competitors.

He later moved into a District Sales Manager role, where his experience in marketing, contract negotiation, and working efficiently with third party vendors proved successful.

He later purchased his second home in Washington, D.C.

Now that he's back home in Honolulu, he looks forward to helping you find your dream home.
